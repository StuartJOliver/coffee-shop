How to find us

We’re conveniently located just outside Elk Mill Retail Park.
Whether you’re a local resident or a visitor exploring the area, here’s how to reach us:

  1. By Car
    • Take the M62 motorway and exit at Junction 20
    • Follow the A627 (M) signposted for Oldham.
    • Elk Mill Retail Park is approximately 1½ miles north of Oldham town center.
    • Look for the Broadway intersection.
  2. Public Transport
    • The nearest bus stop is just a short walk away.
    • Check local bus routes for the most convenient option.
  3. By Car
    • Use our interactive Google map below to get precise directions: View on Google Maps.

While you’re here, take a stroll around Elk Mill Retail Park.
You’ll find a variety of stores, including Argos, Asda Living, Boots,
Currys/PC World, JD Sports, Next, and more.
After shopping, unwind with a cup of our freshly brewed coffee!
